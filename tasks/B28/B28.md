## Produce a Cyber Safety Flyer

### Description

I created a cyber safety flyer targeted at university students about the importance of backup strategies and protecting academic data. The flyer provides simple and practical cybersecurity advice on cloud storage, password protection, and safe backup practices to help students avoid data loss from device failure, malware, or accidental deletion.

### Reflection

As a university student myself, this activity made me realise how vulnerable my own academic work is if I do not regularly back up my files. I often keep assignments, lecture notes, and projects on my laptop without thinking about what could happen if my device failed or became infected with malware. Creating this flyer encouraged me to improve my own backup habits by using cloud storage and keeping multiple copies of important work. It also made me understand how small cybersecurity practices can have a big impact on preventing stress and data loss for students.
